Frequently Asked Questions

1. How does the recommendation site work?

Our site allows you to select preferences like genre and media type (books, movies, or anime), and then we provide a list of recommendations based on ratings and reviews available online.

2. Do I need to create an account to use the site?

No, you donâ€™t need to create an account. Our website is completely login-free, and you can access the recommendations directly.

3. What genres are available for recommendations?

We offer a wide range of genres including fiction, non-fiction, romance, drama, action, comedy, and more for books, movies, and anime.

4. Where do the recommendations come from?

The recommendations are based on APIs from trusted sources, such as Google Books for books, Jikan for anime, OMDb for movies, and other platforms providing user ratings and reviews.

5. Can I suggest a book, movie, or anime for the site?

Currently, we don't have a feature for user suggestions. However, we are always open to feedback and ideas, and we may consider adding this functionality in the future!

6. Are the ratings reliable?

The ratings come from renowned external sources. For books, we use Google Books API, for movies we use OMDb, and for anime, we use the Jikan API, which is based on MyAnimeList. However, we recommend checking the full reviews on the original platforms as well.

7. How can I search for a specific genre?

Simply select the desired genre in the preferences form and click the button to generate recommendations. The site will fetch the best options for you.

8. Is there any cost to use the site?

No, the site is completely free for all users.

9. How can I report an issue with the site?

If you encounter any problems or have suggestions, please contact us via the email provided in the contact section of the site.
